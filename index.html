About me

I'm a secondary English teacher with 13 years of experience — including 9 years at an international school and a role as a certified IPSLE Oral Examiner. I know what proficient English sounds like, and I know how to help learners get there. Outside the classroom, I write about everyday life, personal growth, and the small things most people walk past without noticing. My writing lives at Substack and WordPress, and I'm building a freelance practice rooted in teaching, content, and genuine storytelling.
